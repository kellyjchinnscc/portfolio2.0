Major Web Developer Skill Demonstrated: Client Service



Role

For this particular role, I was a part of a team at a Staffing company called Carecor Health Services. I was part of a team of 7 staffing professionals who had to be in constant contact with clients on a daily basis to assess their staffing needs and our ability to fill those needs.


Skills Gained and Used:

  • Maintaining client contact with honesty to build and solidify relationships.
  • Working with team members to increase shift fill rates by 5% over 3 months, increasing revenues as a result.
  • Communication etiquette over phone and email.

    • Lessons Learned

    This role helped to develop my non-technical skills which seem to be under-valued somewhat in the tech industry by most soon-to-be grads. Having this experience is a blessing, especially when combined with my now-developing tech skills, so that I can bridge the gap in communication between clients and tech teams that I may end up working with.
